# HeyGen Avatar Explainer Template

## Input

Article packet Raw Conversational Edit and 60-Second Video Script.

## Hook

Open direct to camera with the strongest claim.

## Scenes

1. Direct hook.
2. Explain why it matters.
3. Name the system move.
4. Invite the audience to stay close.
5. CTA.

## Captions

Use short sentence fragments aligned with intentional pauses.

## Visual Direction

Calm, direct, high-signal delivery. No exaggerated sales tone.

## CTA

If this resonates, stay close. Because here, we build systems, not just ideas.
